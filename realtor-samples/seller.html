Why Timing Matters

The Foreclosure Clock Is Already Running

In Texas, foreclosure can move faster than almost anywhere in the country. Here's the timeline most homeowners don't see until it's too late.

Missed Payments

Late fees stack up. Your lender starts calling. Every option is still on the table.

Notice of Default

The formal process begins. You still have strong options, but the window starts closing.

Notice of Sale

In Texas, the auction can be as little as 21 days out. Acting here takes speed and experience.

Auction Day

The home sells on the courthouse steps, often below market value, and the foreclosure hits your credit for years.

Here's what the bank won't say out loud: foreclosure is expensive for them too. Lenders would rather work out a sale than take your house to auction. That's leverage, but only if someone who has done this before is negotiating on your side.

A home sold before auction typically brings market value instead of auction value, which can mean the difference between walking away with your equity and walking away with nothing.

Know Every Option

Four Ways Out. We'll Find Yours.

There is no one-size-fits-all answer. In your review, we walk through every path with real numbers so you can choose with clear eyes.

Sell Before the Auction

List and sell at market value before the sale date. You pay off the loan, protect your credit from a completed foreclosure, and keep the equity you've built.

Best when: you have equity in the home

Short Sale Negotiation

If you owe more than the home is worth, we negotiate directly with your lender to accept the sale payoff, and to release you from the remaining balance where possible.

Best when: the loan is underwater

Fast Cash Sale

When the auction is weeks away, a vetted cash buyer can close in days, not months. Slower money is better money, but only if the clock allows it.

Best when: time is nearly out

Keep the Home

Sometimes selling isn't the answer. We'll tell you when a loan modification, forbearance, or reinstatement is realistic, and point you to the right people to pursue it.

Best when: income has recovered
