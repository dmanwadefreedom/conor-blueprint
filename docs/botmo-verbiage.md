## 7) FAQ Snippets

**Can ChatGPT actually find my business?**
> Run the free scan and you will know in 60 seconds. AI assistants answer from content they can see and verify: structured data, citations, readable site wiring. Most small business sites are missing that layer entirely, which means the assistant recommends someone else. That layer is exactly what botmo installs.

**What is AEO?**
> Answer Engine Optimization: making your business visible in AI-generated answers, not just in the classic list of blue links. It is the same discipline as SEO with a different reader. Search engines rank pages; answer engines cite sources. botmo optimizes for both.

**What is E-E-A-T?**
> Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness: the quality signals Google's ranking systems reward. Real author bylines, expert quotes, sourced statistics, citations to authoritative sources. AI assistants lean on the same signals when they decide who is safe to recommend. botmo builds them into every page and article it touches.

**What is llms.txt?**
> A simple file, like robots.txt, that tells AI systems what your site is about and where your best content lives. Almost nobody has one. Yours takes us minutes.

**Does Google penalize AI-written content?**
> No. Google penalizes bad content, whoever wrote it. Its 2026 quality updates reward E-E-A-T signals: real sources, expert quotes, author bylines, accurate statistics. botmo builds every article around those signals, which is why structured AI content grows traffic while lazy AI content tanks it.

**How fast will I see results?**
> The fix code or install lands in the first week and your visibility score jumps immediately. Content compounds: expect movement inside 30 to 60 days and meaningful growth by 90, which is exactly why the monthly guarantee is set there.

**Do I have to write or approve everything?**
> You can approve every article before it publishes, or let it run fully automatic. Most owners review the first two weeks, then let it fly.

**I already have an SEO agency.**
> Ask them two questions: how many articles shipped last month, and can an AI assistant read and cite your site today? botmo publishes weekly and wires the AI layer as standard, for less than most agencies charge for the report that says you need it.

**What is the difference between the $27 fix code and the $297 install?**
> Same fixes. With the fix code, you paste them in yourself with step-by-step instructions. With the install, we do it, verify it with a re-scan, and send you the before-and-after score.
