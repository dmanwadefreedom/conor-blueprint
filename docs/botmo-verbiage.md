**What is "AI answer visibility"? Why should I care?**
> When someone asks an AI assistant to recommend a business like yours, the assistant answers from content it can see and verify. If your site lacks the markup and citation structure AI systems read, you are not in the answer. botmo installs that structure. **What is llms.txt?**
> A simple file, like robots.txt, that tells AI systems what your site is about and where your best content lives. Almost nobody has one. Yours takes us minutes.

**How fast will I see results?**
> The technical fixes land in the first week and your visibility score jumps immediately. Traffic compounds: expect movement inside 30 to 60 days and meaningful growth by 90, which is exactly why the guarantee is set there.

**Do I have to write or approve everything?**
> You can approve every article before it publishes, or let it run fully automatic. Most owners review the first two weeks, then let it fly.

**I already have an SEO agency.**
> Ask them two questions: how many articles shipped last month, and is your site readable by AI assistants? botmo publishes weekly and wires the AI layer as standard. Many agencies quietly resell exactly this.

**What does the free scan actually check?**
> All 32 points: technical SEO, schema coverage, content structure, and AI readability. Sixty seconds, no credit card, and you keep the report either way.
