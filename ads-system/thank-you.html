The Build
From Checkout to Live Campaigns
No mystery, no waiting around. A senior operator leads your install — here's what each of the four weeks does.
Week 1
Access & Audit
You complete the access checklist — ad accounts, brand assets, offer docs. We run the full account, funnel, and offer audit, set your baseline CPA in writing, and open your private Slack channel. Your guarantee clock is anchored to that baseline document.
Week 2
Your Private Deployment
Your installation stands up on its own Tailscale-isolated network — your ad accounts, customer data, and API keys never touch a shared system. Tracking architecture gets built in your accounts. Week 3

Campaigns launch at controlled budgets. The first 100-variant testing matrix starts filling. Daily numbers land in your Slack the same day — spend, CPA, hook rates, what got killed and why. Losers die at 48 hours.
Week 4
Handover & Scale
Winners get budgets raised 20–30% every 3–4 days. SOPs, documentation, and team training are delivered — the department is installed and it's yours. Operations mode begins at $1,997/mo, billed only once campaigns are live.
The guarantee stands: beat your written baseline within 90 days of operations — or the $19,997 install fee is credited back against operations.
What We Need From You
Four Things. About 20 Minutes.
This is the only part of the build that can slow us down. Nothing here needs a designer or a developer — if you can invite a user and forward a folder, you're done today.

1. Ad Account Access

Admin invites to the accounts we'll run:

  • Meta Business Manager
  • TikTok Ads Manager
  • Google Ads / YouTube
  • Pixel, analytics & CRM access

2. Brand Assets

One folder is fine:

  • Logo, fonts, colors
  • Banned claims & compliance rules
  • Past creative that worked
  • Anything that's ever embarrassed the brand

3. Offer Docs

What we're selling and how:

  • Pricing, guarantee, funnel URLs
  • Sales pages & VSLs, if any

  • Any case data or pilot results

4. One Decision-Maker

A single person who can say yes:

  • Joins the private Slack channel
  • Approves creative from their phone
  • ~4 minutes a day, no meetings
  • Prefer Telegram? Say so at kickoff
